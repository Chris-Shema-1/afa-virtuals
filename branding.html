The audit also includes a 90-day ROI projection, so you’ll know the value of automation before we begin.

How does your 90-day implementation process work for real estate teams?

We follow a proven 12-week rollout built around real estate workflows:

Weeks 1–2: Discovery & Strategy – We map your lead flow, property management, and client communication processes.
Weeks 3–6: Foundation Setup – Core automations like lead routing, appointment scheduling, and listing alerts go live.
Weeks 7–10: Advanced Automation – We add automations for follow-ups, CMA generation, reporting, and team notifications.
Weeks 11–12: Optimization & Training – We fine-tune for your market, ensure everything runs smoothly, and train your team.

By day 90, your brokerage has a streamlined, automated system that saves hours weekly and helps close more deals.
